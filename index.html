Our Story

Animelts is a company that I created to sell my perler bead creations. It was created in February 2020. To date I have sold around 15 projects and have donated over 200 face mask holders to healthcare providers in our area. The dream of Animelts is to make products that make people happy and to give back most of the profit back into my community.
